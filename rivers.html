Godavari River

  • The Godavari is India's second longest river after the Ganga and third largest in India, drains about 10% of India's total geographical area.

  • Basin size: 312,812 km2 (120,777 sq mi)

  • Length: 1,465 km (910 mi)

    Krishna River

  • The Krishna River is the fourth-largest river in terms of water inflows and river basin area in India, after the Ganges, Godavari and Brahmaputra.

  • Basin size: 258,948 km2 (99,980 sq mi)

  • Length: 1,290 km (800 mi)approx.

    Penna River

  • The Penna river has several sources and mouths. The main stream starts in Nandi Hills in of Karnataka, flowing for 597 km to the north and east

  • Basin size: 55,213 km2 (21,318 sq mi)

  • Length: 597 km (371 mi)


    Thungabhadra River

  • The Tungabhadra River is a river in India that starts and flows through the state of Karnataka during most of its course, before flowing along the border

  • Basin size: 71,417 km2 (27,574 sq mi)

  • Length: 531 km (330 mi)

    Nagavalli River

  • The River Nagavali also known as Langulya is one of the main rivers of Southern Odisha and Northern Andhra Pradesh States in India

  • Basin size: 4,462 km2 (1,949 sq mi)

  • Length: 256.5 km (159.4 mi)approx
